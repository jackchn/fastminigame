How to Play

8-Ball is a classic pool game played with sixteen balls: a cue ball, seven solid-colored balls (numbered 1-7), seven striped balls (numbered 9-15), and the black 8-ball.

  1. Players take turns shooting, aiming to pocket either all solid or all striped balls.
  2. After pocketing all your assigned balls (solids or stripes), you must pocket the 8-ball to win.
  3. Be careful! Pocketing the 8-ball before clearing your assigned balls, or pocketing it in the wrong pocket, results in an immediate loss.

Controls

Use your mouse or touchscreen to aim. Click and drag to adjust the power of your shot. Release to shoot. The game provides helpful guides to assist with aiming and planning your shots.
